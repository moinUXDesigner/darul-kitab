Cancellation and Refund Policy

This policy explains how subscription cancellations, renewal cutoffs and refund requests are handled for our digital services.

Last updated: 08 April 2026 Digital subscription service

1. Nature of the Service

Quran Fahmi Audio App primarily provides digital audio and subscription-based access. Because access is delivered electronically and may begin immediately after purchase or mandate authorization, refund eligibility is assessed differently from physical goods.

2. Subscription Cancellation

  • You may cancel your recurring subscription from the app, through the payment provider where supported, or by contacting us.
  • When a subscription is cancelled after successful authorization, access typically continues until the end of the already-paid billing period unless otherwise stated at checkout or required by the payment network.
  • Cancellation generally prevents future renewals but does not automatically reverse a billing cycle that has already been successfully processed.

3. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered in cases such as:

  • Duplicate charges for the same subscription period.
  • Successful payment but failure to provide paid access for a sustained period due to a service-side issue.
  • Wrongful or unauthorized charge verified after review.
  • Charge processed after a valid cancellation request where our records show the cancellation should have taken effect earlier.

Refunds are generally not provided for:

  • Partial use, non-use or change of mind after access has been granted.
  • Failure to cancel before the next renewal date.
  • Delays or failures caused by unsupported devices, user-side connectivity issues or third-party platform restrictions outside our reasonable control.

4. Request Timeline

Please raise refund-related requests as soon as possible and preferably within 7 days of the charge date. Delayed requests may still be reviewed, but resolution may depend on available records, payment network rules and legal retention requirements.

5. Review Process

  1. Email moinshaik@quranfahmi.in with your registered email, phone number, payment date, amount and transaction reference.
  2. We may request additional verification to prevent fraud and protect account security.
  3. Eligible refunds, if approved, are usually sent back to the original payment source where possible.

6. Refund Credit Timeline

Once approved by us and initiated through the payment partner, the final credit timeline depends on your bank, card network, UPI app or payment provider. Typical turnaround may range from 5 to 10 business days, though some methods may take longer.

7. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you believe a charge is unauthorized, contact us first so we can review and attempt a direct resolution. You may also use remedies available through your payment provider or issuing institution, subject to their rules and timelines.
