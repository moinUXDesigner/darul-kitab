Shipping and Exchange Policy

Quran Fahmi Audio App is a digital content platform. This page clarifies how delivery works and when exchange or replacement concepts do or do not apply.

Last updated: 08 April 2026 Digital delivery only

1. No Physical Shipping

We do not currently sell or ship physical products. Access to the service is delivered digitally through the website, app interface and associated account-based features.

2. Digital Delivery and Activation

  • Free features become available upon account access, subject to platform availability.
  • Paid features are generally activated after successful payment or successful subscription authorization, though some recurring payment methods may require a processing window or bank-side confirmation.
  • If digital access is delayed because of payment verification, bank processing or payment network timelines, activation may take additional time.

3. Exchange Policy

Since we provide digital access rather than physical goods, product exchange is generally not applicable. If a plan mapping, billing setup or access state is wrong due to an internal error, we may correct the account, update the subscription status or offer another appropriate operational remedy.

4. Delivery Issues

If you were charged but did not receive access, please contact us with:

  • Registered email address
  • Phone number
  • Payment date and amount
  • Payment reference or transaction ID

We will review the transaction and, where appropriate, activate access, correct the account state or process a refund according to the Cancellation and Refund Policy.

Important note

No courier, shipping fee or physical dispatch is involved for current offerings on Quran Fahmi Audio App.
