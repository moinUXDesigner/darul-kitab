2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the service, we may collect:

  • Account data such as your name, email address, phone number and login credentials.
  • Subscription and payment metadata such as plan selected, transaction status, payment gateway references and billing history.
  • Technical and device data such as browser type, IP address, approximate location inferred from IP, device identifiers, operating system, app version and usage logs.
  • Usage data such as listening activity, saved items, playlists, downloads, progress tracking, search history and feature interactions.
  • Communication data such as feedback, support requests and notification preferences.
  • Push notification tokens and consent status where notifications are enabled.
Payment card, UPI PIN and similar sensitive payment credentials are not stored by us. Payments are processed by authorized payment service providers such as Razorpay, subject to their security and privacy controls.

  • To create and manage your account.

  • To process subscriptions, verify payments, prevent abuse and maintain transaction records.
  • To communicate service updates, security notices, support responses and transactional messages.
  • To improve product performance, reliability, personalization and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations, enforce our Terms and protect users, the service and third parties.

For users in India, we aim to align our handling of personal data with applicable Indian legal requirements, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, the Information Technology Act, 2000, related rules on reasonable security practices, and applicable consumer protection and e-commerce requirements.

For users in the EEA, UK or other jurisdictions with similar privacy frameworks, we generally rely on one or more of the following legal bases where applicable: performance of a contract, legitimate interests, legal obligation, and consent for specific features such as marketing communications or certain notification settings.

5. Sharing of Data

We may share limited data with:

  • Payment processors and banking partners for billing, fraud prevention and subscription management.
  • Hosting, analytics, messaging, notification and cloud infrastructure providers acting under contractual controls.

We retain personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including account continuity, subscription servicing, fraud prevention, audit needs, tax and accounting records, dispute handling and legal compliance.

  • Technical logs may be retained for security, diagnostics and abuse monitoring for limited periods.

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration and destruction. No system can guarantee absolute security. Users should also keep their credentials confidential and contact us immediately if unauthorized account activity is suspected.
